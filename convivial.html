🌱 Balcony Gardening Guide

Learn how to grow plants step-by-step

🪴 How to Plant a Plant

  • 1. Choose a suitable pot with drainage holes
  • 2. Add soil mix (garden soil + compost)
  • 3. Place the seed or plant gently
  • 4. Cover roots with soil
  • 5. Water lightly
  • 6. Keep in sunlight

💧 Watering Guide

  • • Water in morning or evening
  • • Do not overwater plants
  • • Check soil before watering
  • • Use clean water

☀️ Sunlight Tips

  • • Most plants need 4–6 hours sunlight
  • • Keep indoor plants near windows
  • • Avoid strong afternoon sun

🌿 Best Balcony Plants

  • • Tulsi
  • • Mint
  • • Aloe Vera
  • • Tomato
  • • Coriander

🌟 Pro Tip

Always check soil moisture before watering. Overwatering is the most common mistake!
